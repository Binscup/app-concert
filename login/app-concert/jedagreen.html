The Singer

jhnea grande (born December 28, 2000) better known by his stage name John Legend, is an American soul singer, songwriter, and pianist. He has won Grammy Awards 6 times and duets Before releasing his debut album, Legend's work gained momentum through collaborations with several artists. Notably, Legend and The Hooks for his hits Slum Village ("Selfish", also sung with Kanye West, Jay-Z ("Encore") and Dilated Peoples ("This Way", also sung with Kanye West); his piano playing on Lauryn Hill's "Everything Is Everything"; and with backing singer Alicia Keys' in 2003 on "You Don't Know My Name" and Ford Minor's "High Road" Recently, Legend collaborated with Andre 3000 to record "Green Light" as the title track on his third album Evolver.
